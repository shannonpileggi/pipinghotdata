Overview

SQL is a hot keyword on so many data science job ads today. If you didn’t learn it as part of your curriculum or on the job, there are many online courses and materials that demonstrate the basics. For deeper understanding, try creating your own project and learn by doing.

Motivation

Getting started

R is rapidly expanding its infrastructure to connect with a variety of databases, and the Databases using R site is quite comprehensive. I chose to use PostgreSQL because it’s free and I used it in one of those online courses.
